1️⃣ Cardiac Rehabilitation
What is Cardiac Rehabilitation?

Cardiac Rehabilitation is a medically supervised program designed to help individuals recover after:

Heart attack

Angioplasty or bypass surgery

Valve replacement

Heart failure

High blood pressure with cardiac risk

It combines structured exercise, lifestyle coaching, monitoring, and risk reduction strategies to improve heart strength and prevent future complications.

Who Needs It?

Patients recovering from heart surgery

Individuals with blocked arteries

People with uncontrolled blood pressure

Those with family history of heart disease

Patients with chest pain or reduced stamina

How Our Program Works

At Pranavayu, cardiac rehab is not just exercise. It includes:

✔ Comprehensive Cardiac Assessment

Blood pressure monitoring

Heart rate variability analysis

Oxygen saturation tracking

Functional capacity testing

✔ Structured Exercise Therapy

Low-impact monitored training

Gradual intensity progression

Heart-safe endurance building

✔ Risk Reduction Protocol

Diet guidance

Stress management

Smoking cessation support

Weight management

Benefits

Improved heart strength

Reduced risk of second heart attack

Better stamina and breathing

Lower blood pressure

Improved confidence and quality of life
