PATIENT JOURNEY
A Structured Path to Measurable Recovery

At Pranavayu Rehabilitation Center, healing is not random.
Every patient follows a carefully designed, clinically guided recovery pathway.

From first consultation to measurable progress, we ensure clarity at every step.

STEP 1: Initial Consultation & Clinical Evaluation
Understanding Your Condition in Detail

Your journey begins with a comprehensive assessment under expert supervision.

We evaluate:

Medical history

Surgical history (if applicable)

Current symptoms

Functional limitations

Oxygen saturation levels

Cardiopulmonary performance

Neurological function (if required)

This ensures we understand not just your diagnosis — but your current physical capacity.

Outcome of Step 1:
A clear clinical profile and recovery roadmap.

STEP 2: Diagnostic & Functional Testing
Measuring Before We Begin

Depending on your condition, we may conduct:

Cardiovascular endurance testing

Lung function evaluation

Oxygen utilization analysis

Balance and gait assessment

Circulatory efficiency screening

These objective measurements help us design a precise program.

Why this matters:
We treat based on data, not assumptions.

STEP 3: Personalized Program Design
Your Condition. Your Capacity. Your Protocol.

Based on your evaluation, we create a structured rehabilitation plan that may include:

Cardiac rehabilitation modules

Pulmonary conditioning sessions

Neurovascular activation therapy

Oxygen-based therapeutic support

Strength and endurance training

Each plan is:

Phase-based

Progress-monitored

Outcome-oriented

STEP 4: Supervised Therapy Sessions
Guided Recovery in a Controlled Environment

Your sessions are conducted under monitored conditions to ensure:

Safe exercise intensity

Stable oxygen levels

Proper breathing techniques

Correct movement patterns

We gradually increase intensity as your strength improves.

Patients are continuously observed for safety and effectiveness.

STEP 5: Progress Monitoring & Reassessment
Measuring Improvement

We periodically reassess:

Stamina levels

Oxygen efficiency

Cardiovascular response

Balance and coordination

Symptom reduction

This ensures visible, measurable progress.

If needed, your program is modified for optimal outcomes.

STEP 6: Lifestyle Integration & Long-Term Guidance
Recovery Beyond the Clinic

True recovery continues outside therapy sessions.

We provide guidance on:

Activity planning

Nutrition awareness

Breathing techniques for home

Stress management

Preventive cardiovascular care

Our goal is not temporary improvement — but long-term stability.
